Sakamoto

A wallpaper of 坂本(さかもと) (Sakamoto) from 日常(にちじょう) (Nichijou).

I'm always looking for nice wallpapers to add to my collection, but I couldn't find one of Sakamoto with the quality I wanted. I also wanted to try my hand at vectoring again, so I decided to give it a try. I used Inkscape and while I did have some trouble with crashes, it performed quite well! Although I don't consider myself an artist or even an imaginative person, I'm quite happy with how this turned out.

Embarrassed Anime Girl

A vector of an Embarrassed Anime Girl

Since the Sakamoto wallpaper, I thought I'd try doing an anime character that is less flat. I stumbled across the original sketch and saw an opportunity to test my skills. I used Inkscape again and figured out that the crashing would happen when I used Ctrl+Scroll when the mouse was over a node. Save often!
I tried some shadows on the body using the blur feature in inkscape. I had some issues where I wanted to retain the transparent background but the blur would go outside the body, and haven't managed to figure it out. Please comment on the DeviantArt link if you know how!
